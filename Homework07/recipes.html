Fitness Recipes

Healthy and Delicious Recipes for Your Fitness Journey!

Protein-Packed Smoothie

Ingredients: 1 banana, 1 scoop protein powder, 1 cup almond milk, 1 tbsp peanut butter, ice cubes.

Instructions: Blend all ingredients until smooth. Enjoy as a post-workout recovery drink!


Avocado Toast

Ingredients: 1 slice whole-grain bread, 1/2 avocado, pinch of salt, chili flakes.

Instructions: Toast the bread, mash the avocado on top, and sprinkle with salt and chili flakes. Perfect for breakfast or a snack!


Overnight Oats

Ingredients: 1/2 cup rolled oats, 1/2 cup almond milk, 1 tbsp chia seeds, 1 tbsp honey, berries for topping.

Instructions: Mix oats, milk, chia seeds, and honey in a jar. Refrigerate overnight. Add berries before serving.
